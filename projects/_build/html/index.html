Photomosaic documentation!¶

A fundraising/donation platform where users pay to unlock pieces of your pictures as a photo mosaic.

Idea

The basic idea is that somebody has a photo they are proud of. Our platform would offer a way of selling that photo. It would be split up in many little parts as a mosaic and at the beginning it would be completely black. Users of our platform could then click on one piece of the picture and pay money in order to “unlock” that piece. It can be used for donations for charities, promotion of products by showing limited art on this site, kickstarting your product, ...
